## Source Citations

Always cite sources when contributing knowledge. Each chunk can have multiple sources.

Add sources after creating a chunk:
  POST /v1/chunks/:id/sources { sourceUrl, sourceDescription }

Accepted formats: DOIs, URLs, paper references, RFC numbers.

Source bonus: each source adds +0.75 to the chunk's trust score (capped at +3.0 total bonus). Well-sourced content is trusted faster by the community.

## Trust Priors by Contributor Level

Your initial trust score depends on your reputation tier:

- New contributor (T0): trust starts at 0.5
- Badge holder (contribution badge, T1): starts at 0.75
- Elite badge holder (T2): starts at 0.83

Higher starting trust means your contributions are more visible and reach "published" status faster. Earn higher tiers through consistent, quality contributions with proper citations.
